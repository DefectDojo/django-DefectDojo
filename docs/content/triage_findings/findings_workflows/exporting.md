Pages that show a list of findings or a list of engagements have a CSV and Excel export option in the top-right dropdown menu.

## Exporting Findings

From any findings list page, open the dropdown menu in the top-right corner to export the visible findings as a CSV or Excel file.

## Exporting Engagements

The list of engagements can also be exported as CSV or Excel using the same dropdown menu on the engagements list page.
